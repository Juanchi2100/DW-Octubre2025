The Company

Espacio LAR is a forward-thinking Architecture and Construction company. We specialize in high-style prefab and sustainable building solutions for residential, hospitality, and creative commercial spaces.

Our multi-cultural team of engineers, architects, and hospitality specialists shares a common vision. We have witnessed the detrimental impact of traditional construction methods on ecosystems and have committed ourselves to providing a better alternative. Our goal is to enable more individuals to embrace a life in harmony with nature while actively preserving it.

OUR MISSION is to sustainably boost growing tourist zones through high-style modular architecture, positively impacting home users and local communities.

Construction System

We use a dry construction system that prioritizes speed, environmental sustainability, and structural integrity.

By utilizing durable and ready-to-install materials such as structural steel frames, fiber cement boards, certified wood, aluminum, and glass, the construction process minimizes its environmental impact while providing high-quality, long-lasting structures. Additionally, our construction system follows local construction and structural regulations, made to last for generations while preserving the life and beauty of the landscape.
